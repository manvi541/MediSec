Our Mission

Empowering healthcare technology against cyber threats

MediSec is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to bridging the knowledge gap in healthcare cybersecurity. We believe that education and collaboration are the keys to protecting sensitive patient data and critical medical infrastructure. We focus on spreading vital information about cyber vulnerabilities and the potential for data breaches that can occur in healthcare systems, ensuring students and professionals are well-equipped to defend against these threats.
