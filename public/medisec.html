Our Mission

Empowering healthcare technology against cyber threats

MediSec is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to bridging the knowledge gap in healthcare cybersecurity. We believe that education and collaboration are the keys to protecting sensitive patient data and critical medical infrastructure. We focus on spreading vital information about cyber vulnerabilities and the potential for data breaches that can occur in healthcare systems, ensuring students and professionals are well-equipped to defend against these threats.

Workshops & Training

Hands-on sessions to build practical cybersecurity skills for healthcare environments.

Research & Publications

Supporting students in conducting and disseminating cybersecurity research.

Join the MediSec Initiative

Whether you're a high school student, college student, healthcare professional, or cybersecurity expert, there are many ways to get involved with our mission to secure healthcare technology.

Why Join MediSec?

  • Gain valuable skills in the high-demand field of healthcare cybersecurity.
  • For High School Students: Build a strong resume for college applications, gain practical experience, and explore a potential career path.
  • For College Students: Enhance your academic profile, find internship opportunities, and network with industry professionals.
  • Contribute to meaningful initiatives that protect patient data and critical infrastructure.
  • Access exclusive training, workshops, and research opportunities.
